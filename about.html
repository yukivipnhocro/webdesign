FPT Software - FPT Software Company Limited

FPT Software is another name for FPT Software Co., Ltd. with the main task of software outsourcing in Vietnam and abroad

Established since 1988 until now with 3 FPT Software Headquarters located in Vietnam and some countries around the world such as the United States, Japan, Malaysia, Germany, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand and Philippines, present FPT Software. meet the needs of large software outsourcing for more than 150 leading companies in the world's 20 largest countries today with large contracts sometimes reaching 1 million USD for a number of major partners such as Hitachi, NEOPOST , Petronas, Deutsche Bank, and Unilever with over 4,000 employees worldwide



FPT Software pursues the goal of software outsourcing to meet the demand for IT development of domestic software firms, domestic companies and ambitious rather than software export worldwide for companies. foreign investors know about FPT Corporation, the main purpose is to reach new heights through technology to improve labor productivity


Coming to FPT Software, employees are allowed to maximize their creativity and efforts to bring innovations into information technology solutions to capture innovation and development opportunities to achieve the common goal of Group is OneFPT - the leading software export company in Vietnam


FPT Sofware's business originating from FPT Information Technology and Telecommunications is the core to meet customers in 63 provinces and cities throughout Vietnam and expand to global customers.
