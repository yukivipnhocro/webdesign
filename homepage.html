2. FPT appointed the new general manager

The Board of Directors of FPT Joint Stock Company (code: FPT) has just approved the appointment of Mr. Nguyen Van Khoa - FPT Deputy General Director to the position of general director, replacing Mr. Bui Quang Ngoc. This decision takes effect from March 29. Mr. Nguyen Van Khoa, born in 1977, is one of the young leaders of FPT. He joined FPT in 1997 with his first role as a staff to deploy and support Vietnamese Intelligence network.
